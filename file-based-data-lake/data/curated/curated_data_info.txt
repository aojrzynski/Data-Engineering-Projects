This folder holds aggregated and analysis-ready datasets.

Characteristics:
- Summarized and optimized for reporting and analytics.
- Structured for easy querying (e.g., Parquet format).
- Ready for use by analysts and business users.

Examples:
- `category_sales.parquet` (total sales by category)
- `monthly_sales_trend.parquet` (monthly sales trends)
- `top_selling_products.parquet` (top 5 products by sales)
